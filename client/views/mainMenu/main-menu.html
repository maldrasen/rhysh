Content Warnings

AI Generated Art
This game makes extensive use of AI generated art. While I understand that people have reasonable ethical objections to the way AI art generators are trained, I view it as a valuable tool. Without it, this game wouldn't have had any graphics, and probably would not have been possible.
Rape
I do not condone sexual violence, this game however does feature quite a lot of it. Rhysh is not a porn game. It's an erotic fantasy/horror adventure game that explores many of the darker sides of sexual fantasy. I don't expect that everyone will feel comfortable with the content herein. My purpose in fact is to make the player uncomfortable. To horrify, shock, and disgust.
