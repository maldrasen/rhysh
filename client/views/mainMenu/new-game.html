The Black Knight is a fearsome armored warrior whose defensive fighting style is focused on disabling opponents with grappling techniques and counter attacks. While his persistent fear aura keeps his followers in line, it also ensures that he will always be more feared than loved.

The Slavers are cruel and cunning rogues who prefer to attack from the shadows, letting their many slaves put themselves in danger while they uses their skills to cripple their opponents.

The Cultists of Rahm are feared across the world for their ability to capture and convert people to their strange eldritch religion. Their unspeakable gods grant them otherworldly powers but require sacrifice to remain in their favor.

The Mindbenders pursue a dangerous school of mind control magics that have been banned in most civilised nations. While they also learn some telekinetic spells for basic defense their real power comes from their fanatically devoted followers.

The Dominatrix delights in the suffering of others, learning a variety of skills to both inflict pain and to inspire people to worship her. She is an agile and deadly combatant when she chooses to be, though often she'd let her devoted slaves do the fighting for her.

The eldritch gods of the Cult of Rahm have chosen this one to be a vessel. While the cultists act as a conduit for the unspeakable gods' power, the Chosen are the physical embodiments of corruption. They wield many of the same otherworldly powers, though often in a more physical way.
