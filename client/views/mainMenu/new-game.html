Strong, agile, naturally magical, and long lived, it's easy to see why the elves have become the dominant species now that the humans have gone extinct. The other species see the elves as arrogant and cruel, a reputation that isn't undeserving as most of the other species had been enslaved by the elves at some point in their histories.
The nymphs are a race of nature spirits known for their unquenchable sexual appetites. Over the course of their long lives they develop animalistic traits, usually a tail or ears, that reflect their personalities. While there are no male nymphs, a few become futanari who (along with their large harems) keep the nymph population stable.
The orcs are a species of proud nomadic warriors. The bad blood between orcs and elves, the result of centuries of enslavement by the elves, keeps most orcs from ever leaving their tribal lands. Those that do are highly sought after by the guilds for their strength, skill, and inexhaustible stamina.
The minotaurs were created long ago in yet another attempt by the ancient elven magi to create the perfect slave race. Though their heads are distinctly bull shaped, with long horns and thick necks, from the waist down they're far more horse like, with everything that implies.
The dragonkind are the most naturally gifted of all the civilized people. In addition to being magically adept and blessed with the ability to breathe fire and having naturally armored bodies, they are stronger, faster, and toughter than almost all the other species. Only their distrust of their fellow dragonkind keeps their power in check.
The lupin were once elves, but the curse of the moon goddess has turned them into unnaturally strong wolf like creatures. The curse gives them the ability to quickly heal from most wounds, but on the full moon of every month they enter into a murderous rage that compells them to consume someone close to them.
The only things that the satyr take seriously are hedonism and debauchery. They share many traits in common with their cousins the nymphs, though they tend to prefer a more physical than magical approach. Many cities forbid satyrs from entering due to their inhibition lowering auras that have a tendency to leave orgies in their wake.
